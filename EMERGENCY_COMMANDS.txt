EMERGENCY COMMANDS

Restart Sameer AI Manager:
systemctl restart sameer_ai_manager

Logs:
journalctl -u sameer_ai_manager -n 100 --no-pager

Status:
systemctl status sameer_ai_manager --no-pager

Compile:
python3 -m py_compile bot.py

Salama Restart:
systemctl restart salama_radiator_bot

Travel Bot Restart:
systemctl restart freshtiq_travel_bot

Rollback Rule:
backup -> compile -> restart -> verify
