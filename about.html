Our Story

FRESHTIQ INNOVATIONS (OPC) PRIVATE LIMITED was founded with a single mission — to make advanced technology accessible to every Indian business, regardless of size or budget. From a small workshop in Azamgarh to serving 500+ clients across the country, we have grown into a trusted name in AI automation, software development, and digital transformation.

Our brand, Freshtiq Automation, represents our commitment to delivering fresh, innovative, and high-quality automation solutions. Whether it's a kirana store in Delhi needing inventory management or a tech startup in Bangalore requiring AI chatbots, we bring enterprise-grade solutions to businesses of all sizes.

Registered as a One Person Company (OPC) under the Companies Act, 2013 with CIN: U47912UP2025OPC236875, we are fully compliant with Indian regulations and tax laws (GSTIN: 09AAGCF7185A1ZL, PAN: AAGCF7185A).
