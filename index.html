FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Can't find what you need? Chat with us.

Q1: What services does Freshtiq Automation offer?

We offer website development, mobile app development, AI chatbots, WhatsApp automation, custom ERP & CRM systems, travel portals, e-commerce platforms, API integrations, data scraping, cloud hosting setup, AI agents, and business process automation.

Q2: How long does it take to build a website?

Most standard websites are delivered within 24–48 hours of payment confirmation. Complex projects like e-commerce sites or custom portals typically take 3–7 days depending on the requirements.

Q3: Do you offer AI chatbot development?

Yes! We specialise in AI chatbots for WhatsApp, Telegram, and websites. Our bots handle customer support, lead generation, order booking, FAQs, and more — powered by GPT and custom AI models.

Q4: What is Sameer AI Manager?

Sameer AI Manager is our central AI admin controller that manages all bots, workers, backups, revenue tracking, and automation workflows from one dashboard. It's the brain behind our automation ecosystem.

Q5: Can you build mobile apps?

Absolutely. We build Android & iOS apps using modern frameworks with beautiful UI/UX, push notifications, backend APIs, and admin panels included.

Q6: What is AutoPilot Hub?

AutoPilot Hub is our customer automation platform that handles project tracking, payment collection, lead management, job workflows, and client communication — all integrated with Telegram for real-time updates.

Q7: Do you provide ERP systems?

Yes, we build custom ERP systems for inventory management, HR & payroll, finance & accounting, production planning, supply chain, and more. EL SALAMA AI ERP PRO is our flagship enterprise ERP.

Q8: What is your pricing model?

We offer transparent project-based pricing starting at ₹999 for basic websites, ₹3,999 for business solutions with app/bot integration, and ₹12,999+ for custom enterprise-grade systems. No hidden fees.

Q9: How do I start a project?

Just message us on WhatsApp at +91 8381848389 or start a chat on Telegram at @AutoPilotHubBot. Tell us your requirement, and we'll share a quote within minutes.

Q10: Do you offer WhatsApp automation?

Yes! We build WhatsApp automation solutions including auto-reply bots, order management, broadcast messaging, lead capture, and full CRM integration with WhatsApp Business API.

Q11: What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers (SBI), UPI payments, and direct deposits. Our SBI account details and UPI ID are listed on our website. All payments are secure and verifiable.

Q12: Is GST invoice provided?

Yes, every client receives a valid GST invoice. Our GSTIN is 09AAGCF7185A1ZL. Business clients can claim GST input tax credit.

Q13: Do you offer support after delivery?

Yes, all plans include post-delivery support — 1 month for Starter, 3 months for Business, and 6 months for Enterprise plans. Extended support is available on request.

Q14: What is the AI Bot Factory?

AI Bot Factory is our rapid bot creation system that generates and deploys custom Telegram/WhatsApp bots for clients in under an hour. It's part of our internal automation toolkit that powers fast delivery.

Q15: Can I get a demo before purchasing?

Absolutely! We offer free live demos for all our products. Just WhatsApp us your interest and we'll walk you through the features with a live demonstration.

Q16: What is the delivery timeline?

Basic projects are delivered in 24–48 hours. Medium projects (dynamic websites, bots, apps) take 3–7 days. Enterprise ERP/CRM systems take 7–21 days depending on complexity.

Q17: Do you work with international clients?

Yes, we serve clients across India and the GCC region (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, Kuwait, Bahrain). International payments are accepted via bank transfer or UPI.

Q18: Can I customize my ERP/CRM?

Yes! All our ERP and CRM systems are fully customizable to match your business workflows. We build from scratch or modify existing templates to fit your exact requirements.

Q19: What technologies do you use?

We use Python (Flask, Django), JavaScript/Node.js, React, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Telegram/WhatsApp APIs, Google APIs, OpenAI/GPT, HTML/CSS, and cloud platforms (VPS, AWS, Railway).

Q20: How do I contact support?

You can reach us via WhatsApp at +91 8381848389, email at freshtiqinnovationspvt@gmail.com, or through our Telegram bot @AutoPilotHubBot. We typically respond within 2 hours.
