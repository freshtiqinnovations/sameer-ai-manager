FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build an automation? ▼

Most projects go live within 5-10 days. Simple websites in 3-5 days. Complex ERP systems 2-4 weeks. We work in agile sprints with weekly demos so you see progress at every step.

Do I pay everything upfront? ▼

No. We work on milestone-based billing. You pay as each section is completed and approved. Typically 25-25-25-25 split across discovery, build, deploy, and handover.

What if I need changes after delivery? ▼

All projects include a revision period and support window. Post that, we offer maintenance packages or one-off change requests at reasonable rates.

Can I see a demo before paying? ▼

Absolutely. Book a free demo on WhatsApp. We'll show you our live CRM, customer portal, and WhatsApp bot in action. No commitment needed.

Do you serve clients outside India? ▼

Yes. We actively serve clients in Saudi Arabia (SAR) and UAE (AED). Pricing is adjusted for local markets. International payments accepted.

What is the reseller program? ▼

Refer clients to us and earn commissions on every project. No tech skills needed — we handle the entire delivery. You get a dashboard to track your referrals and earnings in real-time.
