No qualification meant sales team wasted time on unqualified prospects. The business needed a central brain that captured every lead, evaluated it instantly, and routed it to the right person. Every. A lead management AI system with auto-capture from all sources, AI-powered qualification, scoring engine, and smart routing — all connected to a unified dashboard.
